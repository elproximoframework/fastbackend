## Construction Details

Booster 20 is one of the first units manufactured entirely in the **Starfactory**, SpaceX's new mass-production facility. Unlike earlier units built in temporary hangars, B20 benefits from automated assembly lines and robotic quality controls, reducing manufacturing times from months to just a few weeks.

This unit represents the "frozen" design of the V3 architecture for high-cadence launches. B20 is expected to be a cornerstone of the fleet that will begin in-orbit propellant transfer tests, a fundamental step for Starship to travel to the Moon and Mars. Its structure is optimized for hundreds of catch-and-relaunch missions, with a near-total focus on industrial operability over experimentation.

## Process Innovations

- **Starfactory Assembly:** Utilization of advanced manufacturing techniques for millimeter precision in chassis welds.
- **V3 Standardization:** Elimination of any experimental components; every system is validated by previous flights (B12 through B19).
- **Artemis Readiness:** Will be part of the essential logistical fleet for NASA's crewed missions.
