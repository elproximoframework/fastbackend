## History and Mission (IFT-12 Prep)

Booster 19 is the first V3-series Super Heavy booster to enter active service. It incorporates all structural corrections learned after the failure of B18 and is the flagship of the 2026 flight campaign.

In March 2026, B19 made history as the first booster to perform a successful static fire at the new **Launch Pad 2 (Pad 2)** at Starbase. This new pad features a massive flame trench designed to handle the immense heat of the Raptor 3 engines without undergoing the extreme wear of the original pads. B19 is currently being prepared for the IFT-12 flight, where it is expected to demonstrate the V3 system's ability to perform back-to-back catches with industrial reliability.

## Innovations and Legacy

- **Raptor 3 Debut:** First booster to perform dynamic testing with the final version of the engine without external thermal shielding.
- **Pad 2 Integration:** Validation of the new sound suppression system and flame trench.
- **Payload Leap:** Its configuration allows Starship V3 to reach its target of 100 metric tons of payload in fully reusable mode.
