# Asteroid Mission - ESA

The European Space Agency (ESA) has announced an exciting new mission to study asteroids near Earth.

## Mission Objectives
- Map the surface of binary asteroids.
- Analyze mineralogical composition.
- Test deflection technologies in case of potential impact.

This mission represents a crucial step in our ability to defend the planet against space threats.

![ESA Asteroids](/src/assets/imagesnew/esa_asteroids.png)

### Technical Details
The probe will use state-of-the-art ion propulsion and carry high-precision instruments developed by consortia across Europe.
