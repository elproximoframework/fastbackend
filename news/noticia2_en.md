# SpaceX Starship: Fifth Successful Test

SpaceX has achieved a historic milestone with the fifth orbital flight test of Starship.

## Key Achievements
- Reached low Earth orbit (LEO).
- Controlled atmospheric reentry of the Booster.
- Precise splashdown in the Indian Ocean.

"This is an incredible day for the future of space exploration," commented the SpaceX technical team after recovering the telemetry data.

### What's Next
Elon Musk has indicated that the next test will include an attempt to catch the booster with the arms of the "Mechazilla" tower.
