# Unstoppable Expansion at Starbase and Cape Canaveral for Starship's Future

The infrastructure surrounding the Starship program is expanding at breakneck speeds. At Starbase, Pad 1 is being gutted and rebuilt with deep foundations and high-pressure cement injections to match the superior capabilities of Pad 2. Meanwhile, the Star Factory is rapidly fusing with the Gigabay to create a massive monolithic assembly building.

The action extends to Florida, where preparations have begun at Launch Complex 37 to build a fourth orbital tower. With Pad 39A already conducting active tank and vaporizer tests, SpaceX is laying the groundwork needed to support multiple daily launches.
