# Pad 2: "Gateway to Mars" and Infrastructure Improvements

The Pad 2 launch complex at Starbase has undergone a spectacular transformation. With the return of the iconic "Gateway to Mars" sign, SpaceX reaffirms its long-term vision. Additionally, magnetic proximity sensors have been installed on the booster's quick disconnectors, automating a process that previously required manual intervention.

The tower's "chopsticks" have also been upgraded, replacing the hydraulic system with electric actuators for increased speed and precision. These improvements are essential for achieving the launch and catch cadence required for future routine Starship flights.
