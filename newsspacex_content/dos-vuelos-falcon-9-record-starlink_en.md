# SpaceX Showtime: Two Falcon 9 Flights in One Day and a Starlink Record

April 14 was a busy day for SpaceX. Showcasing its routine yet staggering operational cadence, the company launched two separate Falcon 9 rockets—one from Florida in the early morning and another from California at night.

These launches added 54 Starlink satellites to the megaconstellation, pushing the active tracker past an astounding 10,200 units. The California mission marked SpaceX's 46th orbital flight of 2026, further asserting its absolute dominance over global space access.
