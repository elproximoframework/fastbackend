# Starship Flight 12: First V3 Block with 33 Raptor 3 Engines Roars to Life

On April 15 at 18:35 local time, SpaceX made history by performing the first static fire of the complete Version 3 configuration. Thirty-three Raptor 3 engines roared together on the new Pad 2 at Starbase for 5 seconds.

This new architecture features larger tanks and engines with a massive thrust of 280 tons each. Notably, the bottom of Booster 19 has no heat shield between the engines, relying entirely on regenerative cooling integrated directly into the Raptor 3.

The conservative test of this radically new hardware gives the green light for the imminent Flight 12.
