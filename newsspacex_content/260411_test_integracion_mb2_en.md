# Integration Test at Megabay 2: Booster 20 and Ship 39

SpaceX conducted an unusual physical integration test at Megabay 2, a building typically reserved for starship assembly. The forward section of Booster 20 was moved to the facility to perform interface testing with Ship 39.

This procedure aims to validate that the new Block 3 architecture—featuring a redesigned hot-staging ring and optimized plumbing for Raptor 3 engines—fits perfectly. Detecting mechanical incompatibilities at ground level prevents critical delays during stacking at the launch pad.
