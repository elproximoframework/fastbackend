# Project Terrafab: The Giant Chip Alliance in Texas

SpaceX, Tesla, and xAI have announced an unprecedented collaboration with Intel for the Terrafab project in Texas. The goal is to build the world's largest semiconductor factory, capable of producing one terawatt of computing power per year.

This initiative seeks to ensure technological sovereignty for Elon Musk's companies by manufacturing radiation-hardened chips for Starlink, AI processors, and electronic brains for Tesla's robots. The ability to produce proprietary hardware is key to the vision of Mars colonization, where dependence on Earth-based supplies must be kept to a minimum.
