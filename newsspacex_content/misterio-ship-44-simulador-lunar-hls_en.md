# The Mystery of Ship 44 and Starbase's Lunar Simulator

While Ships 40, 41, 43, and 45 are moving along production predictably, the space community has noticed one glaring absence: Ship 44, missing since September 2025.

Leading theories suggest it was not scrapped, but rather stowed away as the premier test article for the Human Landing System (HLS) program. Adding fuel to the fire, a massive barrel segment equipped with lander leg mockups has been spotted in Starbase's southern lots, hinting at early landing simulator trials for Artemis.
