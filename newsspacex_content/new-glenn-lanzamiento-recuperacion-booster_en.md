# Blue Origin's New Glenn Preps Third Launch to Attempt Booster Recovery

Slated for April 19, Blue Origin's sleek and massive New Glenn rocket is preparing for its third orbital flight (NG-3), carrying an enormously large Bluebird 7 satellite.

Cargo aside, the true objective lies in Blue Origin's historic attempt to recover and reuse the first-stage booster, affectionately named 'Never Tell Me The Odds'. While their operations remain a fraction of what SpaceX is putting forward with Starship, it marks the opening salvos of true heavy-lift commercial reusability competition.
