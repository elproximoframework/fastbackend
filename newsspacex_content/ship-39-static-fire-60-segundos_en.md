# Ship 39 Stuns with a 60-Second Static Fire at Massey's

On the night of April 14, Ship 39 fired its six Raptor 3 engines for an impressive 60 seconds at the Massey's test site. The facility's flame bucket easily withstood the immense energy.

This burn validated previous cryogenic proofing tests as well as the long-duration reliability of the Raptor 3 engines under simulated flight conditions.

Curiously, Ship 39 also sported large patches of thermal tiles on its leeward side. SpaceX is using the ship as a flying laboratory to test tile adhesives against acoustic vibrations during liftoff.
