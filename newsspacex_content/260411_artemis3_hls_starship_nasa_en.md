# HLS Starship: The Artemis III Challenge and NASA's Budget

The success of Artemis II places immense pressure on SpaceX for the Artemis III mission, which aims to put humans on the Moon for the first time since 1972. Although NASA has cut its 2027 budget by 23%, the HLS (Human Landing System) program has been shielded as the agency's top priority.

Before the lunar mission, SpaceX must demonstrate critical milestones, such as in-space cryogenic refueling. This will be attempted following the historic orbital Flight 13, validating Starship's capability to act as a reliable interplanetary transport system.
