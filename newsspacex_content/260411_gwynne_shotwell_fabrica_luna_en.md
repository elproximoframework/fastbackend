# Gwynne Shotwell: A Factory on the Moon in 10 Years

SpaceX President Gwynne Shotwell has revealed ambitious plans for establishing a lunar settlement and a manufacturing base within the next decade. The company intends to use lunar regolith to manufacture components and satellites directly on the Moon's surface.

This strategy not only supports lunar colonization but also turns the Moon into a strategic shipyard. Leveraging the Moon's low gravity, satellites are planned to be launched using electromagnetic catapults, drastically reducing costs and validating the technologies necessary for the ultimate goal: Mars.
