# Explosion at McGregor: Raptor 3 Put to the Limit

SpaceX's facilities in McGregor, Texas, witnessed a colossal explosion on April 6th during a Raptor 3 engine test. The Rapid Unscheduled Disassembly (RUD) occurred after approximately 30-70 seconds of successful operation, reducing the engine to scrap.

While spectacular, such incidents are often part of SpaceX's "limit testing" strategy. The Raptor 3 is designed for 280 tons of thrust, but the company is pushing to reach 300 tons. With mass production rates and significantly lower costs than traditional engines, SpaceX uses these failures to identify the real limits of its hardware.
