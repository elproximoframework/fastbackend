# Booster 19 Completes Wet Dress Rehearsal, Validating Pad 2

On April 16, SpaceX capped off a successful week of testing with a full Wet Dress Rehearsal of Booster 19 on Pad 2.

The giant Version 3 tanks were frosted over as the Ground Support Equipment (GSE) loaded thousands of tons of liquid oxygen and methane at a frantic pace of 45 tons per minute. This validated not only the rocket but the entire loading infrastructure of the new complex.
