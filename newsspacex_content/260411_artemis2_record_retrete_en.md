# Artemis II: Distance Record and the Frozen Toilet

NASA's Artemis II mission was a resounding success, with the Orion capsule surpassing the historic human distance record from Earth set by Apollo 13. However, the mission was not without its curious challenges: the waste management system froze due to the extreme cold on the far side of the Moon.

To solve the problem, the crew had to rotate the entire spacecraft and expose it to the sun to thaw the pipes. This incident highlights the technical challenges of prolonged deep-space missions and the importance of creative solutions for unexpected problems.
