# Falcon Heavy Returns with Viasat-3

Later this month, SpaceX's heavy-lift stalwart will return to the Florida skies. The Falcon Heavy is set for a comeback flight following an October 2024 hiatus to execute a critical mission: hauling the massive Viasat-3 comsat.

The energetic requirements needed for a deep Geostationary Transfer Orbit will force SpaceX to fully expend the rocket's center core in order to maximize payload performance. However, spectators at the Cape will still enjoy the traditional dual synchronized landing of the side boosters.
