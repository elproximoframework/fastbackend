# SpaceX Goes Public: 2 Trillion Dollar Valuation

Leaked reports indicate that SpaceX has filed a confidential application with the SEC to prepare for an Initial Public Offering (IPO), targeting a valuation of up to two trillion dollars. If successful, this figure would shatter the historical fundraising record set by Saudi Aramco.

The astronomical valuation is not based solely on rocket launches, but on Starlink's absolute monopoly and Starship's disruptive potential for global cargo transport. SpaceX could become the world's sixth-largest company, competing directly with giants like Apple, Nvidia, and Microsoft.
