# Flight 12: Booster 19 Prepares as the First Full Super Heavy V3

Booster 19, the first Super Heavy rocket fully equipped with 33 third-generation Raptor engines, has already completed its first static fire test at Pad 2. Elon Musk confirmed that Flight 12 is scheduled for May 2026, marking the debut of the Block 3 architecture for both stages.

This flight aims to stress the new hardware to its limits, including the heat shield, redesigned fins, and new engines. Unlike Flight 13, Flight 12 will maintain a suborbital profile, similar to Flight 11, while validating critical version 3 improvements.
